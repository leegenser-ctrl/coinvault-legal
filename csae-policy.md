# CoinLocker — Child Safety Policy

## Our Commitment to Child Safety

CoinLocker has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to providing a safe environment for all users and strictly prohibit any content or behavior that exploits or endangers minors.

## Prohibited Content and Behavior

CoinLocker strictly prohibits:
- Any content that sexually exploits minors
- Any content that depicts child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Any grooming behavior targeting minors
- Any attempt to use the platform to exploit or harm children

## User Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates this policy:
- Use the in-app Contact & Report Issues button in the Profile screen
- Email us directly at myvirtualcoinvault@gmail.com with subject line "CSAE Report"
- We will investigate all reports immediately and take appropriate action

## Enforcement

Violations of this policy will result in:
- Immediate account termination
- Reporting to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC)
- Cooperation with law enforcement authorities

## Age Requirements

CoinLocker requires users to be at least 13 years of age. Users under 18 must have parental consent to use the app.
