# CoinLocker — Account Deletion Request

## Delete Your Account In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the app:

1. Open CoinLocker
2. Tap **Profile** in the bottom navigation
3. Scroll down and tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion

This immediately and permanently deletes your account and all associated data including your locker, photos, messages, and profile.

## Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:

**myvirtualcoinvault@gmail.com**

Please include:
- The email address associated with your account
- Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email.

## What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted the following data is permanently removed:
- Your profile and display name
- All coins and patches in your locker
- All photos you uploaded
- Your chat history and messages
- Your match history
- Your ISO alerts
