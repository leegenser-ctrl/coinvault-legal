# Privacy Policy — CoinLocker

**Last updated: May 2026**

CoinLocker ("we," "us," or "our") operates the CoinLocker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.

**User Content**
Photos you upload of your coins, patches, and collectibles are stored securely in our cloud storage.

**Usage Data**
We collect information about how you use the App, including items added to your vault, swipe interactions in the Discover feature, and messages sent through the chat system.

**Device Information**
We collect your device's push notification token to send you alerts about matches and messages.

**Purchase Information**
If you subscribe to CoinLocker Pro, purchase information is processed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App
- Enable the trading and matching features between collectors
- Send push notifications for matches, messages, and ISO alerts
- Process and manage Pro subscriptions
- Respond to your support requests

## Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We share information only with:

**Firebase (Google)** — We use Firebase for authentication, database storage, and photo storage. Firebase's privacy policy is available at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

**RevenueCat** — We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

**Apple** — In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy is available at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at lee.genser@gmail.com.

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase's built-in security rules and encrypted data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

CoinLocker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
